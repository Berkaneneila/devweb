Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days (Monday to Friday). During peak seasons, response times may extend to 48 hours.

Do you offer customized tour packages?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized travel experiences. Our experts will work with you to design an itinerary that matches your interests, pace, and budget.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and cash payments in our office. For international clients, we also accept PayPal and other digital payment methods.

Can I modify my booking after confirmation?

Yes, modifications are possible depending on availability. Please contact us as soon as possible to discuss changes to your booking.
